Our Website allows Penn State Harrisburg Community to reserve a parking Spot at any Penn State Harrisburg Parking Lot. We are constantly adding new Parking Lots on out site which eliminates the time required to look for a Parking Spot.

About Us

A Penn State Group dedicated to Solve the Parking Problem. Penn State has more than 5 parking lots in which each parking lot has more than 50 spots. We save users time by allowing the users to reserve one of the few parking spots around the campus parking. This saves time for the user as they can simply park their car once reserved. We will add more Parking Spots in near Future.

How it Works?

Go to the Map and reserve a Parking Spot. Enter the number of Hours you want to park your car. Add money to your account in the pay tab. When you arrive at the lot, be sure to bring a smart phone. It will be needed to check in and out. You will be charged for the duration of your stay according to the price listed on the map. Be sure to maintain a positive balance in the pay tab or you will be unable to use our services again in the future. If you have any complaints, report an issue by clicking the ! icon next to your reserved parking spot.
