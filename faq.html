Frequently Asked Questions

If you are hesitating, do not worry - we are here to explain
everything you might want to know. Let us help!

Why is Parking Simplified Useful?

Parking Simplified eliminates the time required to find a parking spot by allowing the user to reserve a Parking Spot. Moreover, the rising popularity of autonomous vehicles creates a new use for parking management systems. In the future, the entire reservation and parking process may be carried out by the autonomous vehicle's software. The vehicle would be able to reserve a spot in our system and correctly navigate to and park in the reserved spot all on its own.

Who can use our Application?

It can be used to manage the Penn State Harrisburg parking lot or any parking lot in the world.

What to do if someone parks in my spot?

Simply go to the map and click the ! button. In the form that pops up, enter the model and license plate number of the vehicle.

What happens if my Time Limit expire?

You will receive a text message to extend your parking spot.

Do you offer 24/7 support?

No, the Office Hours are: Monday - Friday, 10:00 am to 5:00 pm.
